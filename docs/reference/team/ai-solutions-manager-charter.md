# AI Solutions Manager Charter

## Role Snapshot

- Role: AI Solutions Manager
- Team shape: New Pod with 4-5 members
- Direct report: AI Engineer
- Horizon: 3-6 month product incubation cycles

## Core Responsibilities

- Product and delivery orchestration for rapid AI product experiments.
- Hands-on development and architecture support.
- Establish AI-first engineering standards and reusable patterns.
- Ensure execution speed without sacrificing reliability and security.

## Decision Rights

- Prioritize Pod backlog and milestone sequencing.
- Define technical standards for AI workflows in Pod repositories.
- Set expectations for documentation and decision traceability.

## Success Metrics

- Time from concept to internal prototype.
- Time from prototype to pilot-ready build.
- Defect rates and reliability during pilot windows.
- Reuse rate of shared components/prompts/evaluation assets.
- Team throughput and cycle-time predictability.

## Collaboration Interfaces

- Engineering leadership for platform alignment.
- Product stakeholders for opportunity framing.
- Security/compliance for safe AI implementation boundaries.
