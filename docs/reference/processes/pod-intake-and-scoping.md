# Pod Intake and Scoping Process

## Metadata

- Owner: AI Solutions Manager
- Last reviewed: 2026-04-13
- Confidence: High (team operating standard)

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable intake process for selecting and shaping AI Pod bets that
fit a 3-6 month delivery horizon.

## Trigger

- New product idea, stakeholder request, or strategic initiative.

## Inputs

- Problem statement
- Business impact hypothesis
- User/persona target
- Technical feasibility signals
- Constraints (security, compliance, data, timeline)

## Process Steps

1. Intake capture (lightweight one-page brief).
2. Triage (fit score: impact, urgency, feasibility, differentiation).
3. Scope draft (MVP boundary and success metrics).
4. Feasibility spike (timeboxed, usually 1 week).
5. Go/no-go decision with explicit rationale.
6. Convert approved work into sprint-ready backlog.

## Outputs

- Decision record in `docs/reference/processes/decision-log.md`
- Scoped initiative brief in `docs/explanation/` or `docs/reference/`
- Jira epic with acceptance and success metrics

## SLA

- Intake triage within 3 business days.
- Feasibility recommendation within 10 business days for standard requests.

## Escalation Path

- Security/compliance uncertainty -> security owner review
- Core dependency risk -> engineering/platform lead review
- Strategic conflict -> executive stakeholder review
