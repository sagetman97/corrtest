# Compliance Process (AI Pod)

## Metadata

- Owner: Security/compliance partner + AI Solutions Manager
- Last reviewed: 2026-04-13
- Confidence: Medium

## Purpose

Ensure Pod delivery aligns with company and regulatory obligations.

## Trigger

- New product feature with customer, financial, or security implications.
- Major architecture or data-handling changes.

## Inputs

- Feature scope and data flows
- Relevant controls and obligations
- Risk assessment from engineering/security

## Workflow

1. Identify control domains impacted.
2. Validate evidence requirements.
3. Confirm required approvals.
4. Document mitigation and monitoring controls.
5. Record decisions in decision log.

## Outputs

- Compliance notes linked to initiative brief
- Required control evidence checklist
- Go/no-go recommendation for release stage
